About Us

In the pivotal year of 1969, as the world marveled at the historic moon landing, we joyously welcomed the first patron to step into Gary Blue's Diner in St. John's, Newfoundland & Labrador. This marked a significant moment for diner cuisine, akin to a giant leap for hearty meals. News of our delectable offerings spread like wildfire, prompting the establishment of numerous diners across the province, swiftly becoming the preferred dining destination, truly iconic in its own right. The time had come for us to, pardon the expression, broaden our culinary horizons.

Fast forward to 1972, and the tantalizing secret of Gary Blue's Diner had captivated taste buds beyond Newfoundland & Labrador. That year witnessed the opening of two additional establishments in Ontario, making a flavorful entrance in Oshawa and Mississauga. Our journey continued, expanding westward to Alberta, eastward to the Maritimes, reaching the Prairies, and recently venturing all the way to British Columbia and the Northwest Territories.

Today, the Gary Blue's Diner family boasts nearly 300 locations scattered across Canada, with this number steadily ascending. From coast to coast, in quaint towns and bustling metropolises alike, Canadians are falling head over heels for the irresistible flavors of our culinary creations. Buckle up, for we are poised to bring our exceptional dining experience to your neighborhood very soon!
